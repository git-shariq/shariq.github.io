Experience

I am currently working as a Unity Developer at AppKart Studio, where I design and develop engaging mobile and PC games using Unity and C#. I began as an intern in February 2025 and transitioned to a full-time role on 14th August 2025.

My work involves building core gameplay systems, designing intuitive UI/UX, and optimizing performance across devices. I’ve contributed to multiple published titles including Screw Puzzle, Number Riddle, and Pocket Games.

I also developed the complete User Interface (UI) for the horror PC game “Agyaat”, creating immersive menus, HUDs, and interaction elements that enhance the dark atmosphere of the game. Working closely with designers and QA teams, I focus on maintaining consistency, smooth gameplay flow, and high player engagement.

Beyond development, I’m experienced in Play Store publishing, debugging, version control, and maintaining live builds — ensuring each project delivers a polished, high-quality experience.
